Tools That Actually Matter

Why LifePera Exists

Most free tool websites are built for developers — unit converters, code formatters, JSON validators. Useful, but not life-changing. LifePera was built for everyone else: the traveler who wants to know what their passport unlocks, the person questioning a relationship pattern they can't quite name, the professional wondering if they're underpaid.

We believe that access to clear, actionable information — the kind that used to require expensive consultants, therapists, or hours of research — should be free and instant for everyone on Earth.

Every tool on LifePera answers a question real people are actually asking. We built 36 of them, covering travel, relationships, career, psychology, finance, and culture — and we're adding more every month.

Our Core Values

  • Free Forever: Every tool is free to use, unlimited times, with no account required.
  • Radically Private: Your data never leaves your browser. We don't collect your quiz results, your income, or your relationship answers.
  • Globally Inclusive: We build for everyone on Earth — not just people in wealthy English-speaking countries.
  • Honest Results: Our tools give you real, data-driven answers, not just what you want to hear.
