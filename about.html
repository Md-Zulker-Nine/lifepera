Why LifePera Exists

Most free tool websites are built for developers — unit converters, code formatters, JSON validators. Useful, but not life-changing. LifePera was built for everyone else: the traveler who wants to know what their passport unlocks, the person questioning a relationship pattern they can't quite name, the professional wondering if they're underpaid.

We believe that access to clear, actionable information — the kind that used to require expensive consultants, therapists, or hours of research — should be free and instant for everyone on Earth.

Every tool on LifePera answers a question real people are actually asking. We built 36 of them, covering travel, relationships, career, psychology, finance, and culture — and we're adding more every month.

Our Core Values

🔓 Free Forever

Every tool is free to use, unlimited times, with no account, no credit card, and no hidden limits. Always.

🔒 Radically Private

Your data never leaves your browser. We don't collect quiz results, income figures, or relationship answers. Every calculation runs locally via JavaScript.

🌍 Globally Inclusive

We build for everyone on Earth — not just people in wealthy English-speaking countries. Income tools use global percentile data. Travel tools cover every passport.

🎯 Honest Results

Our tools give you real, data-driven answers, not just what you want to hear. We cite sources where possible and are transparent about methodology.

Editorial Standards & Fact-Checking

LifePera is committed to accuracy and transparency. Our content and tool methodologies are based on:

  • Data-driven research: Income percentiles from World Bank, IMF, and national statistics. Travel data from government sources and international databases. Relationship psychology from peer-reviewed attachment theory research.
  • Regular updates: Tool data is reviewed quarterly. Visa-free travel data is updated as policies change.
  • Correction policy: If you find an error, email contact@lifepera.com. We correct verified errors within 5 business days and note the correction.
  • No sponsored content: We do not accept payment to influence tool results or editorial content. Advertisements are clearly marked.

I built LifePera because I kept finding myself searching for tools that didn't exist. The information existed — scattered across academic papers, Reddit threads, and expensive consultants — but it wasn't accessible. So I built it.

Every tool on this site was designed with one question in mind: "Would this genuinely help someone make a better decision today?" If the answer wasn't a clear yes, I didn't build it.

I personally review every tool, every blog post, and every data update. LifePera is my full-time commitment to making useful information free for everyone.
