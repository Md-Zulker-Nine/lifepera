Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026 · Effective: June 10, 2026 · lifepera.com
The Short Version
  • We do NOT collect or store your tool answers, quiz results, or personal data entered into any tool.
  • All tool calculations happen entirely in your browser — nothing is sent to our servers.
  • We use Google Analytics and Google AdSense which have their own privacy practices.
  • We do not sell your personal information to anyone, ever.
  • You can contact us at contact@lifepera.com with any privacy questions.

1. Who We Are

LifePera ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website lifepera.com, a free tools and content website based in the United States. Our contact email is contact@lifepera.com.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

All interactive tools on LifePera — including quizzes, calculators, and checkers — operate entirely within your web browser using JavaScript. No data you enter into any tool is transmitted to our servers, stored in any database, or retained in any form. This includes:

  • Your income figures entered into the How Rich Am I tool
  • Your quiz answers in the Attachment Style, EQ, or personality quizzes
  • Your relationship checklist responses in the Toxic Relationship tool
  • Your email text entered into the Email Tone Checker
  • Any other personal information entered into any tool on this website

3. Information We Do Collect

Contact form submissions: If you use our contact form or email us directly, we receive your name, email address, and message. We use this only to respond to your inquiry and do not add you to any mailing lists without your explicit consent.

Newsletter subscriptions: If you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect your email address to send you weekly updates. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email we send.

Analytics data: We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors use our website. This collects anonymized information such as pages visited, time spent, device type, and general geographic location (country/city level). This data does not identify you personally.

4. Advertising — Google AdSense

LifePera displays advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google uses cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the internet.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.

5. Cookies

Our website uses the following types of cookies:

  • Essential cookies: Necessary for the website to function properly
  • Analytics cookies: Google Analytics cookies to understand site usage (can be opted out)
  • Advertising cookies: Google AdSense cookies for ad personalization (can be opted out)

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at:

Email: contact@lifepera.com
Website: lifepera.com
